Scene One

The room glowed with the soft hum of monitors, green and blue lights blinking like a city that never slept.

Maddox hated silence. It always meant something was about to break.

“You’re late,” she said.

“No,” Maddox replied. “I’m exactly on time.”

Outside, rain pressed against the windows, dragging thin lines downward like tears no one wanted to acknowledge.
